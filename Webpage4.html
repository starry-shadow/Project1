Modernism

Modernism can be summed up in one quote by German-American architect Mies van der Rohe

"Less is more."
-Mies van der Rohe

Modernism came from the industrial revolution which brought new materials and philosophy.
This philospohy was form follows function.
This mean't using new materiels such as steel and hollow,thinner walls to allow for more interior space and had big glass windows.
Many skyscrapers were builting using this idealology.
In 1932 this architectural style became so popular it gave rise to the Museum of Modern Art in New York City.

Examples of Modernism architecture:

Owings & Merrill's Willis Tower

Mies van der Rohe's Barcelona Pavillion

Oscar Niemeyer's Palacio Do Planato
